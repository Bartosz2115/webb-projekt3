About us

Welcome to Zieliński Automobile AB, your ultimate destination for the world of automotive excellence and the art of car collecting. We are passionate enthusiasts who have dedicated our lives to curating a remarkable collection of automobiles that will captivate the hearts of enthusiasts and collectors alike.

At Zieliński Automobile AB, we believe that cars are more than just machines; they are symbols of innovation, craftsmanship, and timeless elegance. Our mission is to provide a platform where automotive enthusiasts can indulge in their passion and explore the beauty and history of these remarkable machines.

Our collection represents a symphony of automotive beauty, carefully selected to showcase the finest examples of automotive design and engineering. From vintage classics that evoke a sense of nostalgia to powerful sports cars that push the boundaries of performance, each vehicle in our collection has been meticulously chosen for its exceptional quality, authenticity, and significance.

But our dedication goes beyond mere curation. We take immense pride in preserving the heritage of these automotive works of art. Our team of experts ensures that each car undergoes a rigorous inspection process, guaranteeing that it meets our uncompromising standards of quality and authenticity. We strive to showcase these vehicles in their pristine condition, allowing enthusiasts to appreciate their timeless allure.

At Zieliński Automobile AB, we go beyond simply collecting cars; we are storytellers. We believe that each vehicle has a unique narrative to tell, whether it's the engineering marvels that pushed the boundaries of their time or the fascinating stories of their previous owners. As you explore our collection, we invite you to delve into these stories and immerse yourself in the rich history and heritage that each car carries.

We are more than just a website; we are a vibrant community of like-minded individuals who share a passion for automobiles. By joining Zieliński Automobile AB, you gain access to exclusive events, engaging discussions, and the opportunity to connect with fellow enthusiasts and experts. Together, we celebrate the allure of automotive excellence and create lasting memories fueled by our shared love for these remarkable machines.

Whether you are a seasoned collector, an aspiring enthusiast, or someone who simply appreciates the beauty of automobiles, Zieliński Automobile AB offers a gateway to an extraordinary journey. Allow us to ignite your passion, inspire your imagination, and guide you through the enchanting world of automotive excellence. Step into our world and experience the thrill of car collecting like never before.

Welcome to Zieliński Automobile AB, where automotive dreams become a reality.
