How can I contact Zieliński Automobile AB?

You can reach us through our contact page on the website or by emailing us at [email protected] We strive to respond to all inquiries promptly.

We selectively consider consignment opportunities based on the uniqueness and quality of the vehicle. Please reach out to us with details about your car for further evaluation.
